About

We founded Semicolon after noticing the gap that exists between what is taught to us, specifically in our colleges, and what is required from us in the industry as developers. We firmly believe in the fast moving pace of our Tech Industry and acknowledge the fact that it takes young creative minds to make an impact on the same. All we want to do, is to make Full Stack Development more accessible to students, with interactive courses we have developed ourselves.

Courses Offered

The whole premise of Full Stack Development is set on the idea that a developer who can proficiently build all fronts of an Application, Web or ortherwise, has a reduced need of dependency on specialized fronts. Therefore, we at Semicolon offer a wide variety of courses that aim at making a programmer handle all kinds of work in our Industry.

Internships

Being engineering students ourselves, we know how important landing internships are. With over 4 years of joint experience in working in the technical industry, we have a network of companies and startups that are interested in hiring trained interns. The Semicolon Initiative is not only about learning new technologies, it's also about applying them in real world live projects. What we have realised about internships is that they are not just activities that last for a couple of months after which they get reduced to words on CVs, internships when done properly amount to gaining Mentors, and being a part of the technical circuit, where lies the real action. So we at TSI, strive to offer this experience to our students right after their course.
